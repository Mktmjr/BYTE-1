About Byte
Everything you need to know
before your first order.

Byte isn't just a food delivery app. It's a health-first platform built specifically for anyone anywhere — combining smart nutrition filtering, real-time vendor data, and a student discount model that actually works.

01
The Problem We're Solving
Students want to eat healthily but face two barriers: price and convenience. Campus cafeterias are expensive. Fast food is cheap but nutritionally empty. Byte closes that gap. Healthy food at student prices, delivered to your campus.
The gap we fill
02
How the Platform Works
Byte aggregates healthy meals from partner vendors into one place. Users browse, order, and track deliveries in one platform. Every meal is screened and scored. Discounts apply automatically for verified students.
Platform model
03
How the Pricing Works
Byte earns a 12% commission from the vendor — not the student. The student discount is co-funded by sponsors who pay for visibility on the platform. Students save money, vendors gain reach, sponsors access the student market.
Win-win model
Vendor
Shares menu database with Byte. Gains customers without building their own app.
⇄
Byte Platform
Aggregates vendor data into a live health-filtered catalogue. Handles orders, payments and delivery.
⇄
Student
Gets a curated healthy menu, automatic student discount, and fast delivery.
⇄
Sponsor
Co-funds the student discount in exchange for brand visibility to an engaged student audience.

Byte Score
We score every meal
so you don't have to.

The Byte Score is our proprietary nutrition rating — a simple 1–10 number calculated from protein, fibre, calorie balance, and ingredient quality. Higher score, healthier meal.

Only meals scoring 7.0 or above are listed on Byte. We do the filtering so you can just order.
